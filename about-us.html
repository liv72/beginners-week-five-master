About us

We are Liv and Lily and are the creators of this website. We have both had an interest in fashion for quite a while. Most importantly we have wondered about some very peculiar fashion trends we have sampled on our homepage.

Our inspiration came from ridiculous outfits seen on the runway. We wondered what people actually thought of these outfits and formulated a plan to get other peoples opinions of these Waki costumes.
